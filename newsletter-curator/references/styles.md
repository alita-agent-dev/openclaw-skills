# Newsletter Style Templates

## Daily Brief

Short, scannable, under 500 words.

```markdown
# 📰 Daily Brief — {date}

*A quick rundown of what matters today.*

---

## Top Story

**{title}**
{2-3 sentence summary}
[Read more]({url})

## Quick Links

- **{title}** — {one-line summary} [→]({url})
- **{title}** — {one-line summary} [→]({url})

---

*That's it for today. See you tomorrow.*
```

## Weekly Digest

Medium length, 800-1500 words. Three distinct sections.

```markdown
# 📬 Weekly Digest — {date_range}

*{editor's note — 2-3 sentences about the week's theme or standout items}*

---

## ⭐ Featured

### {title}
{source} · {date}
{2-3 sentence summary}

[Read full article]({url})

*(repeat for 1-3 featured items)*

---

## 🔗 Quick Links

- **{title}** — {one-line summary} [→]({url})
- **{title}** — {one-line summary} [→]({url})

*(4-8 items)*

---

## 🔍 Deep Dive: {title}

{3-5 paragraph extended analysis of the most interesting item}

[Read original]({url})

---

*Curated with ♥. See you next week.*
```

## Monthly Roundup

Longer format, 1500-3000 words. Categorized by theme.

```markdown
# 📚 Monthly Roundup — {month} {year}

*{editor's note — 3-4 sentences reflecting on the month's trends}*

---

## Highlights

### {title}
{source} · {date}
{2-3 sentence summary}
[→]({url})

*(3-5 highlight items)*

---

## By Topic

### {topic_name}
- **{title}** — {summary} [→]({url})
- **{title}** — {summary} [→]({url})

*(repeat per topic, 2-5 items each)*

---

## Deep Dive: {title}

{5-7 paragraph in-depth analysis of the most significant piece}

[Read original]({url})

---

## Looking Ahead

{2-3 sentences on trends to watch next month}

---

*{items this month}: {count}. See you next month.*
```
