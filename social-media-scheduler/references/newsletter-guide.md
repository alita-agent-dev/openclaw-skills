# Newsletter Blurb Guide

## Format Rules

- **50–150 words** per blurb
- One idea per blurb
- Written to drive clicks (tease value, don't give everything away)

## Structure

1. **Subject hook (5–8 words):** Curiosity gap or bold claim
2. **Context (2–3 sentences):** What's interesting and why it matters
3. **CTA:** "Read more →", "Full story here →", or "Check it out"

## Tone

- Matches newsletter voice (usually more personal than social)
- Direct address ("you") works well
- No clickbait — promise value, deliver it

## Variations

- **Link blurb:** Tease an external article with your take
- **Original blurb:** Summarize your own content with a link to full post
- **Announcement:** Product launch, event, milestone — keep it tight

## Usage

- These blurbs go into email newsletters, not social platforms
- Typically batched (3–5 blurbs per newsletter)
- Archive in content calendar for repurposing
